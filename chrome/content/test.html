aPebble: aPebble is a suite of organizing tools and actions built and successfully used over the course of numerous campaigns and causes to affect change in our world. These tools, we feel, should be available to everyone.

The general idea of these tools is to publish and manage various actions you want people to participate in to help you with your cause or campaign.

In other words:

  • You can create and publish an action (For example, a sign up form or a phone bank).
  • Tell people about it and promote it.
  • They can take action.
  • You can see the results of each others work.

Hey, you might even get other people on aPebble to help you out.

The tools are FREE (that's cool), but it also means they are constantly evolving, require different levels of expertise to setup, and improve every day from the support and feedback of our users.

Also, be aware that things change underfoot and not every tool is right for every cause. If you find something that dosen't work as expected, please let us know at info@apebble.com.
